Dr. Antonio (Tony) Silveti-Falls

Starting September 2022 I will be assistant professor/maître de conférences at CentraleSupélec/University of Paris-Saclay in the Centre de Vision Numérique laboratory.
I am currently a postdoctoral reseracher in applied mathematics at the Toulouse School of Economics advised by Jérôme Bolte and Edouard Pauwels. Before this, I did my PhD at the university of Caen in the GREYC laboratory under Jalal Fadili and Gabriel Peyré.
My research interests: {nonsmooth, infinite-dimensional, stochastic} convex optimization, conservative calculus, applications to machine learning, deep learning, image/signal processing, and inverse problems.


Giving a talk on nonsmooth generalized conditional gradient methods at SPARS 2019
